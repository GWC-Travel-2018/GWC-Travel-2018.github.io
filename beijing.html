Cautions while travelling in Beijing

Taxi drivers will try to rip off travellers. If that happens, tell a hotle and he will be blacklisted.
Beware of the Tea House Scam. Someone will start a conversation, get tea, and leave you to pay for it.
Be careful when crossing the street
For Fire Emergencies, dial: "119"
For Police Emergencies, dial: "110"
For Medical Emergencie, dial: "120"
If you don't speak Chinese or Mandarin, dial: "6525 5386"
