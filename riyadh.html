Caution when travelling to Riyadh

Increased caution due to terrorism and threat of missle attacks on civilian targets
Enroll in the Smart Tracker Enrollment Program (STEP) to recieve alerts and make it easier to locate you in an emergency
Christianity is legal but must be practiced indoors
Stay away from Yemen Border
For Police Emergencies, dial: "999"
For Fire Emergencies, dial: "998"
For Medical Emergencies, dial: "997"
For Traffic Emergencies, dial: "993"
